Fluffy Pancakes

Homemade pancake recipe makes the most delicious and fluffy stack of pancakes around

In a separate bowl,add all the need ingredients and mix it in a bowl. Heat a large skillet Once hot add butter,Add the pancake batter and Cook the pancakes until bubbles and the bottom is golden brown Flip the pancakes and cook on the second side until golden brown

  • AP flour
  • whole milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Salt

once upon a chef
Measurements Ingredients
100 Gms Ap flour
1/4Tsp Baking Soda
1/4 Tsp Salt
1/2 cup Milk
2Tbsp Butter
2 Eggs
As needed Maple syrup
